was

o que; qual; que; algo, alguma coisa ([et]was) - en: what

  • alles was er sagte - tudo que ele disse
  • Alles was ich habe ... - Tudo que eu tenho ...
  • Auf was wartest Du? - O que você está esperando?
  • Ich darf nichts essen, was ... enthält. - Não posso comer nada que contenha ...
  • Ich weiß, was ich will. - Eu sei o que quero. - I know my own mind.
  • Nein, so [et]was! - Que coisa! - Well, I never!
  • Um was geht es? | Um was handelt es sich? - Qual é o assunto? - What"s it all about?
  • Was bedeutet das? | Was heißt das? - O que quer dizer isto?
  • Was bedeutet das Wort? - O que quer dizer esta palavra? | O que significa est palavra? - What does the word mean?
  • Was bringt`s? | Was bringt es? - Qual é a vantagem?
  • Was dann? - E depois? - What then?
  • Was es auch sei, was ... - Seja o que for, que ...
  • Was fehlt noch? - O que está faltando ainda?
  • Was für ...? - Que tipo de ...?
  • Was gibt es Neues? - Que há de novo? - What"s the news?
  • Was halten Sie davon? - Qual a sua opinião (sobre...)? - What do you make of it?; How does it strike you?
  • Was hast du gesagt? - O que você disse?
  • Was ist los? - O que é/foi? | O que está acontecendo? | O que acontece? - What"s the matter? | What"s the pitch? | What"s up?
  • Was ist passiert? - O que aconteceu? - How it happened?
  • Was kostet ...? | Wie viel kostet ...? | Wie viel macht es? - Quanto custa ...? | Qual é o preço ...? | Quanto é ..? - How much is it?
  • Was machst du Sonntag? - O que você faz no domingo? - What do you do on Sunday?
  • Was meinst du? - O que você acha?
  • Was nun? - E agora? - What now?
  • Was sagt er? - Que diz? | O que ele está dizendo?
  • Was Sie nicht sagen! - Não me diga! - You don"t say! | You don"t say so.
  • Was (wer) sonst? - Que(m) senão?
  • Was (wer) sonst noch? - Que(m) mais?
  • Was wünschst/möchtest/willst du? - Que deseja?
  • Was/welches ist dein Beruf? - Qual é a sua profissão?
